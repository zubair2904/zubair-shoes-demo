Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our collection, select your desired product, choose your size and color, then click "Add to Cart." When you're ready to checkout, click on the cart icon and follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placing them. Please contact our customer service team immediately at support@zubairshoes.com or call us at +91 98765 43210.

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package in real-time.

We offer Standard Shipping (5-7 business days), Express Shipping (2-3 business days), and Next Day Delivery. Free standard shipping is available on orders over ₹8,000.

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs fees may apply depending on your country.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original packaging. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact our support team. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

To exchange for a different size, please return the original item and place a new order for the correct size. This ensures faster processing and guarantees the item is in stock.

Check our detailed size guide available on each product page. We recommend measuring your foot length and comparing it to our size chart. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for comfort.

Most of our shoes run true to size, but some brands may vary slightly. Check individual product descriptions for specific sizing notes. Customer reviews can also provide helpful sizing insights.

Care instructions vary by material. Generally, we recommend using a soft brush for cleaning, avoiding direct sunlight when drying, and using appropriate protectants for leather or suede. Check our blog for detailed care guides.
